REFLEX RUSH

1. A ball with a random number (1-4) appears at the bottom and moves upward.

2. Press the correct number key (1, 2, 3, or 4) before the ball disappears.

3. If you press the correct key, you score a point.

4. If you press the wrong key, you get a penalty.

5. If you miss pressing any key, it counts as a missed ball.

6. The game speeds up as you progress, testing your reflexes and accuracy!

Press the N key to toggle sound on and off.
